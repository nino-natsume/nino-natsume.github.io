About my website

This site is built on the Hugo framework using the hugo-theme-reimu theme. It’s a personal blog, and I personally rate it as age 12+. There are a few articles that require careful attention, such as “The Complete Hugo Website Building Process,” which you might recognize if you’ve read it. Yes, 12+ is definitely the right age.

The content on this site might be a bit disorganized; some parts were just things I wrote on the spur of the moment, and some were just random stuff I wrote to test certain functions. You can choose to ignore them.

The site’s name is Jigen Seiki, nothing special. The theme and background generally use anime/manga style, corresponding to the “dimensional” in the name. As for the “starfield” part? I just added it on the spot.

Uh, you ask me the purpose of building this site? As a blog, its intention is to record personal learning content and share anime/manga related materials, etc. Well, I feel like it’s getting closer and closer to the former function, haha.

In the future, I might share anime/manga related materials, but that depends on whether I can collect anything interesting.
